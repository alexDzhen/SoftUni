2.	Grades

•	2.00 – 2.99 - "Fail"
•	3.00 – 3.49 - "Poor"
•	3.50 – 4.49 - "Good"
•	4.50 – 5.49 - "Very good"
•	5.50 – 6.00 - "Excellent"
